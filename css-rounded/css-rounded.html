Tigers, scientifically known as Panthera tigris, are one of the most iconic and revered big cats in the animal kingdom. Known for their striking orange coats adorned with dark stripes, these apex predators have captured the imagination of people across the globe. This essay explores the biology, habitat, behavior, conservation status, and cultural significance of tigers, highlighting their importance in our world.
